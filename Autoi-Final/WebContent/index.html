The Product

Auto-i controller V1.

Small cheap and easy to install. The Auto-i v1 controllers can be very easily installed inside electrical switch boards enabling your devices with Internet control. The controller, after installation can be easily fed with the WiFi use name and password through the App and it would automatically connect with the WiFi of you house enabling your device with all the perks of Seamless control. All this is very simple and can be done in a matter of minutes and the steps are very clearly available in the installation guide that comes with the product.You would also have access to links to the videos explaining the installation very clearly in simple steps. If you still have doubts you can always reach to our competent support line to help you explain things.
